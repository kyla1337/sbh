Deborah Dai'Re

M.S. | LPC-MHSP

Helping others has always been more than just a passion—it’s my life’s calling. I knew I was meant to guide people through life’s struggles, and I dedicated myself to making that vision a reality. With a Master of Science (M.S.) and credentials as a Licensed Professional Counselor – Mental Health Service Provider (LPC-MHSP), I provide a safe, compassionate space for healing, growth, and transformation. My mission is to empower others to overcome obstacles, reclaim their strength, and step into the life they deserve.
