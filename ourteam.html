Computer Science & Technology

Technology has always been a huge part of my life. From an early age, I was captivated by the complex intelligence of early AI that can now in our modern day help us write code. I was never the kind of kid who played with Barbies or dressed up; Instead, I gravitated toward learning programming languages, experimenting with code, and video games. Spending hours immersed in exploring and learning technology.

At around 10 years old, when I got my first computer, I took my love for gaming a step further by creating my own modifications inside of games like Minecraft. That experience sparked a deep passion for building and innovating—whether it’s experimenting with code, enhancing games, or exploring the possibilities of AI. This is just the start of my journey, and I’m excited to keep creating and learning.

Real Estate

This experience allowed me to combine my analytical skills, love for problem-solving, and growing interest in technology to make informed decisions and create meaningful solutions for people with all kinds of service or technical needs.
